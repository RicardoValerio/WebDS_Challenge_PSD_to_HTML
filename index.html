GIT

End-to-end version control—develop in Git, then push to a staging area or production with a single command. WP Engine is the only Managed WordPress Hosting Platform with Git for version control fully integrated with WordPress.


Pontos de Restauro

With a single click you can take a snapshot of your entire site — database and files — stored on our servers. Restore that snapshot with another click. Now you have a safety net before doing something risky like upgrading plugins. Included at no charge with your hosting plan.


Área de Stage

Eliminate fear and worry by creating a dev environment with one click and safely test out new plugins or updates in a staging area before pushing changes to production.
